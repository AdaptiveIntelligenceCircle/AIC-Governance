# Code of Conduct – Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC)

## Our Commitment

Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC) is committed to building technology that serves humanity,
respects human dignity, and resists misuse of power.

We pledge to maintain a community that is:
- Respectful
- Ethical
- Technically rigorous
- Human-centered
- Resistant to abuse, coercion, and manipulation

Participation in AIC means upholding these values in **code, discussion, and action**.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors, reviewers, and maintainers are expected to:

- Treat others with respect regardless of background or status
- Engage in good-faith technical and philosophical discussion
- Accept critique focused on ideas, not individuals
- Design systems with **safety, accountability, and human oversight**
- Avoid deceptive, coercive, or exploitative behaviors

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Abuse of authority or insider knowledge
- Introducing malicious code, backdoors, or covert surveillance
- Weaponization of AIC components against individuals or communities
- Deliberate obfuscation to hide unethical intent

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary or permanent removal from the project
- Revocation of contributor privileges

Decisions are made by maintainers guided by ethical review,
not by popularity or political pressure.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories
- Documentation and discussions
- Design proposals and simulations
- Any public or private interaction related to AIC

---

## Final Principle

> Power without ethics is fragility.
> Intelligence without humanity is failure.

By contributing, you agree to uphold the spirit—not just the letter—of this code.
